Our Sustainable Model

A unique approach to community growth where local families lead the way.

The KETAAKETI Partnership

Mindokatie Salone implements the globally recognized KETAAKETI Financing Concept. Unlike traditional aid, this model focuses on Self-Determination. International partners provide the initial startup capital, but the local Mindokatie team and the village communities decide exactly how to use it.

0% Interest Micro-loans

We provide interest-free loans to women in the poorest families of Koinadugu. This allows them to start small businesses—such as market stalls or farming—without the burden of debt. Because there is 0% interest, the women can reinvest 100% of their success back into their families.

The Cycle of Independence

1

Support

Mothers receive an interest-free startup loan to begin a small business.

2

Success

The business earns profit, allowing the family to become financially stable.

3

Education

Profits pay for school uniforms, books, and shoes, keeping children in school long-term.
