Our Sustainable Concept

Mindokatie Salone operates as a primary partner of KETAAKETI Germany, implementing a sustainable cooperation model for the world's poorest.

The KETAAKETI Partnership

Mindokatie implements a unique financing concept where the poorest families receive support to build independent futures. We follow the core principle of self-determination: our local team in Kabala decides how funds are distributed, ensuring aid is culturally and socially relevant.

This partnership allows for 100% of project donations to reach Sierra Leone, with zero administrative overhead being deducted from donor funds.

0% Interest Micro-Loans

We empower women by providing small, interest-free startup capital for businesses. These loans are not a "debt trap"—they are a bridge to independence. As businesses grow, the profits are used by the families to pay for their children's secondary school costs.

  • No predatory interest rates
  • Focused on female entrepreneurs
  • Local community management

The Cycle of Growth

1

Startup Loan

Mothers in Kabala receive zero-interest capital to launch small-scale market or farm businesses.

2

Family Profit

As the business earns, the family achieves food security and a stable daily income.

3

School Support

Business profits pay for uniforms and books, ensuring the next generation finishes school.
